About Us

The Wideland Group of companies was founded in 2008 and is wholly Australian owned.

Through synergic diversification and specific specialisation in the Agricultural, Construction, Motor Vehicle, Trucking and Vehicle Rental Industries, the Wideland Group of companies are transforming various Australian Market Segments.

The Wideland Group's vision is "To be one of Australia's great companies, who through its' companies, creates wealth and prosperity for our customers, communities and people".

Our strategy seeks to deliver on this vision by providing superior returns for our shareholders; maintaining and building genuine customer relationships, being involved in our communities and being an employer where the best people want to work.
